TAB 0 — Deal-or-No-Deal Filter

Quick 2-minute screen so you don’t waste time on non-deals.
“Before we dive in, I just need to confirm a few quick things so I don’t waste your time or mine — fair?”

Employment:
“Where are you working currently?”

Income:
“What do you typically bring in each month after taxes?”
(Program eligibility requires consistent income.)

Checking Account:
“You still have an active checking account for bills and deposits, right?”

Pay Frequency:
“Are you paid weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly?”

Job Duration:
“Been at the current job at least a month?”

If they clearly do NOT qualify:
• Politely explain eligibility requires stable income and active checking.
• Set a future call if appropriate.

If they qualify:
“Perfect. You’re eligible to continue.”
PlanteMan Method™ – Filter fast, then go all-in.

TAB 1 — Frame & NSL Pre-Credit Disclosure

Establish control, purpose, and comply before pulling credit.
FRAME:
“Hey, this is David with JG Wentworth — this call is recorded for quality.
My role is to review your information and see which options you may be eligible for that could help improve your financial situation. Sound good?”

Verify:
• Address
• Date of birth
• Last 4 of SSN

NSL PRE-CREDIT DISCLOSURE (REQUIRED IN NSL STATES — BEFORE PULLING CREDIT):
“Before we review your credit and determine which options you may qualify for, I need to go over something with you.
One of the potential options you may be eligible for is with New Start Legal — a law firm that provides attorney representation in your state.
Would you like to hear more details about how that works?”

If YES:
• Proceed to credit review and remaining script.

If NO:
“No problem at all. Without reviewing that option, there wouldn’t be anything else I could offer that would realistically help your situation today.
If anything changes, feel free to reach out to me directly. I’m always here to help.”
(End call.)
PlanteMan Method™ – Authority + compliance from second one.

TAB 2 — Pain Discovery (NEPQ)

Get the real story fast, without pressure.
“How did the debt start building up for you?”

“Do you feel like the payments are helping, or mostly going toward interest?”

“How have savings been lately?”

“Are these cards being used day-to-day, or are they mostly just bills now?”

“Any major purchases planned in the next 6 months?”
PlanteMan Method™ – Ask, don’t assume.

TAB 3 — Loan Review / Stick & Move

Crush the loan fantasy compliantly and pivot to reality.
“You’ve got about $____ across ____ accounts — does that look accurate?”

“Based on the current utilization and recent payment history, lenders typically won’t extend additional unsecured loan options.
That’s common when utilization is high.”

(NEPQ Question)
“If a lender DID approve a loan — hypothetically — do you feel it would solve the issue, or might it just shift the balances around?”

(They answer.)

“Right — that’s exactly why traditional loans usually aren’t helpful here.”
PlanteMan Method™ – Break the illusion, open the mind.

TAB 3.5 — Sales Score Impact Presentation

Show them what the system sees, not your opinion.
“Here’s what the system is showing:
• Reported balances
• Minimum payments
• Interest rates
• Estimated portion going toward interest — very common with revolving credit.”

“And here’s your utilization.
High utilization generally makes lenders more cautious about extending new credit.”

“This situation is extremely common.
Revolving credit structures can make it challenging to make real progress.”
PlanteMan Method™ – Reveal, don’t lecture.

TAB 4 — Current Path Consequence

Direct warning, no scare tactics.
“Based on the current minimum payments and interest rates, the system estimates this could extend roughly 28–32 years, depending on changes in usage and rates.
A significant portion of that time is interest.”

“How does seeing that feel on your end?”
PlanteMan Method™ – Make the cost visible.

TAB 5 — Program Review / Approval

You’re not selling a miracle. You’re offering a structured option.
“The system did show eligibility for a hardship-based program designed to help consumers address unsecured debt more efficiently than traditional revolving credit.”

“This is NOT a loan.
It’s a negotiation-based program where results vary depending on creditor policies.”
PlanteMan Method™ – Position the program correctly.

TAB 6 — Program Explanation

Simple, compliant, and clear.
“Here’s how the program generally works:
• The goal is to negotiate reduced payoff amounts on enrolled accounts
• We work directly with your creditors
• Future interest may be reduced or stopped where negotiated
• Your monthly deposits build a balance used for settlement negotiations”

“These are standard debt resolution procedures.
Results vary based on each creditor’s policies.”

“How does this compare to what your lenders have offered?”
PlanteMan Method™ – Clarity beats hype.

TAB 7 — Conditions

Set expectations without killing momentum.
Condition 1 — No Using the Accounts
“Because creditors will be reviewing the accounts for potential reduced payoff options, the enrolled accounts must remain inactive after your first program deposit.
Otherwise they may not qualify. Fair?”

Condition 2 — Temporary Score Movement
“In the early stages, credit scores may take a temporary step back — that’s normal in debt resolution.
Nothing negative is being added; it’s just how reporting works during the review process.
Over time, as accounts resolve, many clients see improvement, but results vary.”
PlanteMan Method™ – Protect expectations, protect your reviews.
